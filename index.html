Frequently Asked Questions

Essential information before using Zella.

Is Zella suitable for beginner traders?
Yes. Zella presents market information in a structured and readable format while still providing advanced tools for experienced traders.
Can Zella execute trades automatically?
Auto trading can be enabled when a supported exchange is connected, the required access level is active and all risk controls are configured.
Does Zella require withdrawal permission?
No. Withdrawal permission should remain disabled on every exchange API key connected to Zella.
Does Zella guarantee profitable trades?
No trading platform can guarantee profits. Zella provides market intelligence and decision-support tools, but users remain responsible for their own trading decisions and risk.
